Research Interests
Control theory        : stochastic control theory; information and control (in decentralization, filtering or information theoretic constraints); robustness and approximations in control; reinforcement learning theory; multi-agent systems
 Information theory : optimal quantization and coding; interaction of information theory, control theory and game theory
 Probability              : Non-linear filtering; learning theory; stochastic stability of dynamical systems
